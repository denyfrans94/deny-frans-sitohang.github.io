Melatih Model¶

1. Training¶

X_train, X_test, y_train, y_test = train_test_split(X,y, random_state=0, test_size=1/3)
In [16]:
from sklearn.linear_model import LinearRegression

regressor = LinearRegression()
regressor.fit(X_train, y_train)
